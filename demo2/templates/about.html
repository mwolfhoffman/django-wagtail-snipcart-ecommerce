Artsy Angler

JEREMY NORDIN-BERGHUIS

Jeremy Nordin-Berghuis, “The Artsy Angler” grew up in small town northern Wisconsin. From an early age, Jeremy developed an interest and appreciation for art. Surrounded by talented artisans and craftsman within his family, Jeremy was introduced to working with wood, metals, fibers, and painting. Driven to understanding and developing his own skills, he actively pursued art, predominantly ceramics, throughout high school and on into adulthood. This led to jobs in clay and print making studios as well as machine shops. In addition to concentrating in art, Jeremy has a deep caring and passion for working with and understanding people. In 2009 Jeremy began working at a summer camp. His interest in working with kids there helped in motivating him towards going back to school. In 2011 Jeremy attended the University of Wisconsin River Falls where he earned his B.S.A. in Art Education with an emphasis in ceramics. Today, Jeremy lives and works in Boise Idaho. It is there that his love and passion for ceramics has developed into a career in making. His work is designed and meant to be held, used, and enjoyed by others around him.
